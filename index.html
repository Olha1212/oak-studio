01 The Studio

Built on
Precision

OAK is a multidisciplinary studio at the intersection of architecture and interior design. We believe that the most powerful spaces are those where every detail — from structural logic to the quality of light at dusk — is considered with equal intention.

Our work spans private residences, boutique hospitality, and cultural spaces. Each project is an original dialogue between client, context, and craft.

04 Our Philosophy
"We do not design spaces — we design experiences of time, light, and inhabitation."

OAK Studio Manifesto

Contextual Integrity

Every project is inseparable from its place. We study site, climate, culture, and material tradition before committing a single line to paper.

Materiality First

Materials are not finishes — they are the substance of experience. We select every surface for how it ages, how it sounds, how it feels in changing light.

Long-Term Thinking

We design for decades. Every decision is weighed not only for its immediate beauty but for how it will be lived in, and cared for, over a lifetime.

05 How We Work

A process built
on trust

Step 01

Discovery & Vision

We begin with deep listening — understanding how you live, what you value, and what kind of space will serve you best.

Step 02

Concept & Design

We develop a concept that responds to place, program, and person — tested through drawing, model, and material study.

Step 03

Development & Coordination

Every detail is resolved — technical, material, and regulatory — coordinating all consultants into a unified vision.

Step 04

Delivery & Handover

We remain on-site throughout construction, ensuring the built work reflects the intent of the design in every detail.
