01   The Studio

Built on
Precision
& Atmosphere

OAK is a multidisciplinary studio at the intersection of architecture and interior design. We design boutique environments for clients who understand that the most powerful spaces are those where material richness, spatial clarity, and atmosphere are considered together — from the first structural decision to the quality of afternoon light.

Our work spans private residences, boutique hospitality, and cultural spaces. Each project begins with deep listening and ends with something that could not have existed anywhere else.

Material Thinking

Every surface
has a memory

We select materials not for their appearance in photographs, but for how they age, how they sound underfoot, how they hold light at dusk. Stone that has been quarried. Timber that has been dried slowly. Plaster that is trowelled by hand.

This is the substance of experience — and it is where OAK begins every project, long before the first line is drawn.
